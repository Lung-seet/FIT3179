Average House Prices of Melbourne between 2016 and 2018

The average house prices of houses is heavily affected by the position and area that the house is situated in. This can be seen through the southeast part of the map, with higher average prices compared to other parts. This could be contributed to things like landmark (beaches, shopping centres), food hotspots (Richmond, South Yarra, Hawthorn).

Price vs Distance

The average house prices of houses is heavily affected by the distance of the house from the city center. The houses closer to the city has a much higher average compared the houses further in the suburbs. This is visualised with the price range of the first 10km with price ranging from $600,000 to $2,500,000 and 30-40km away with price ranging from $400,000 to $1,200,000
